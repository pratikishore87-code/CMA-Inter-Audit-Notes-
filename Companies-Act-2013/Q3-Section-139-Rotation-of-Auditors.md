## Question 3 вҖ” Rotation of Auditors
> **PYQ:** June 2018 | June 2023 (New) | Study Material

### Question
Discuss the manner in which **rotation of auditors** may be done by the company
on expiry of their term.

---

### Answer вҖ” Section 139(4) read with Rule 6 of Company (Audit and Auditors) Rules, 2014

As per **Section 139(4)**, the Central Government may prescribe rules for the manner
in which companies shall rotate their auditors under Section 139(2).

Accordingly, **Rule 6** of the Company (Audit and Auditors) Rules, 2014 prescribes
the following:

---

### Procedure for Rotation

#### Case 1 вҖ” Company WITH an Audit Committee (Section 177)
> The **Audit Committee** shall recommend to the Board the name of an individual
> auditor or audit firm to replace the incumbent auditor on expiry of their term.

#### Case 2 вҖ” Company WITHOUT an Audit Committee
> The **Board of Directors** shall consider the matter of rotation and make its
> recommendation for appointment of the next auditor at the **Annual General Meeting (AGM)**.

---

### Important Notes on Rotation рҹ“Ң

#### Note 1 вҖ” Counting the Term
- The period for which the individual or firm held office as auditor
  **prior to the commencement of the Act** shall be counted when calculating
  **5 consecutive years** (individual) or **10 consecutive years** (firm).

#### Note 2 вҖ” Incoming Auditor Eligibility
- The **incoming auditor or audit firm** shall **not be eligible** if they are
  associated with the **outgoing auditor or firm** under the **same network of audit firms**.
- **"Same network"** includes firms operating under the same:
  - Brand name
  - Trade name, or
  - Common control

---

### Additional Rules for Rotation

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Break in term | A break of **5 continuous years** shall be considered as fulfilling the rotation requirement |
| Partner moves to new firm | If a partner-in-charge of the audit firm retires and joins **another firm**, that other firm is also **ineligible for 5 years** |

---

### Rotation Period at a Glance

| Type of Auditor | Maximum Consecutive Term |
|---|---|
| Individual Auditor | **5 years** |
| Audit Firm | **10 years** |
| Cooling-off period (break required) | **5 years** |

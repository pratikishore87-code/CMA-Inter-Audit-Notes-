## Question 4 — Branch Audit
> **PYQ:** December 2017 | December 2019 | MQP December 2024-II | Study Material

### Question
Write a short note on **Branch Audit**.

---

### Answer — Section 2(14) & Section 143 of Companies Act, 2013

---

### What is a Branch Office?
As per **Section 2(14)** of the Companies Act, 2013:
> A **"branch office"** in relation to a company means any establishment
> described as such by the company.

---

### Who Can Audit a Branch? 

Where a company has a branch office, the accounts of that branch shall be
audited by **any one** of the following:

#### Case 1 — Branch in INDIA 🇮🇳

| Option | Auditor |
|---|---|
| Option A | The **company's own auditor** appointed under this Act |
| Option B | **Any other person** qualified for appointment as auditor under this Act, appointed under Section 139 |

#### Case 2 — Branch OUTSIDE India 🌍

| Option | Auditor |
|---|---|
| Option A | The **company's own auditor** |
| Option B | An **accountant or any other person** duly qualified to act as auditor of branch accounts **in accordance with the laws of that country** |

---

### Duties of Branch Auditor

The branch auditor shall:
1. **Prepare a report** on the accounts of the branch examined by him
2. **Send the report** to the **company's auditor**
3. The company's auditor shall then **deal with it in his report**
   in such manner as he considers necessary

---

### Flow of Branch Audit Process
```
Branch Accounts Prepared
         │
         ▼
Branch Auditor appointed
(Company's auditor OR qualified
 person OR foreign accountant)
         │
         ▼
Branch Auditor examines accounts
& prepares Branch Audit Report
         │
         ▼
Report sent to Company's Auditor
         │
         ▼
Company's Auditor incorporates
findings in the Main Audit Report
```

---

### Key Points to Remember 🧠

| Point | Detail |
|---|---|
| Definition | Section 2(14) — any establishment described as branch by the company |
| Indian Branch | Company auditor OR any qualified auditor under the Act |
| Foreign Branch | Company auditor OR auditor qualified under laws of that country |
| Branch Auditor's duty | Prepare report and send to company's auditor |
| Company Auditor's duty | Deal with branch report in his own audit report |

---

### Key Sections to Cite in Exam 📌
- **Section 2(14)** — Definition of Branch Office
- **Section 139** — Appointment of Auditor
- **Section 143** — Powers and Duties of Auditors (covers branch audit)

---

### Exam Tip ⭐
The most commonly tested point is the **two separate cases** —
branch in India vs. branch outside India — and the role of the
**branch auditor reporting TO the company's auditor**.
Always mention the proviso about the branch auditor's report
being sent to the main auditor.
